Involvement:

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers UIC Chapter

Secretary | Jan. 2018 - Present

Since January of 2018, I have served as the secretary of UIC’s IEEE chapter. Through this position I have assisted in planning and coordinating bi-weekly general body meetings as well as other events such as workshops. I also managed the roster of all our active members and was in charge of sending out periodic emails to the engineering college and our members about the organization’s current events.

Apart from my duties and responsibilities as an officer in the organization, I attended leadership conferences, as well as met with professionals in industry in order to help expand the chapter’s reach and make new connections. Website: https://www.ieee-uic.org/
